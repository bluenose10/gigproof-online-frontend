Key LA Gig Economy Numbers

  • 450,000+ active gig workers in LA County
  • 9% of LA workforce participates in gig economy
  • $4,500-6,500/month average full-time Uber driver earnings
  • $3,800-5,500/month average full-time DoorDash driver earnings

LA's Position: Los Angeles has the largest gig economy in the United States by total workers, accounting for approximately 8% of all US gig workers.
