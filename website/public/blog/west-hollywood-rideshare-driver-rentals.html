Q: Is WeHo good for Uber/Lyft drivers?

A: Excellent. Highest per-capita rideshare demand in LA County. Sunset Strip nightlife, celebrity clientele, and entertainment industry create consistent high earnings.

A: 2.5-3x monthly rent. For $2,800/month, you need $7,000-8,400/month. Full-time dual-platform drivers regularly meet this threshold.

Q: Do WeHo landlords accept rideshare income?

Q: Which WeHo area is best for drivers?

A: East WeHo (La Brea) for best value, Central WeHo for balanced access, Sunset Strip area if you can afford highest rent and want zero commute.

Q: Can I afford WeHo on rideshare income?

A: Full-time dual-platform drivers earning $6,500-8,500/month can qualify. WeHo's high earning potential helps offset higher rent.

Q: What documents do WeHo landlords need?

A: Tax returns, 1099s from Uber and Lyft, 3-6 months bank statements, and professional income PDF. WeHo landlords are generally flexible with gig income.
