Venice-Specific Application Tips

Tip 1: Mention Local Focus

In your cover letter: "I deliver food to the Venice community, primarily serving Abbot Kinney and Rose Ave restaurants. Living locally means faster deliveries and being part of the neighborhood."

Tip 2: Target Individual Landlords

Venice has many individual landlords who own older units. These landlords often understand freelance/gig income better than corporate property managers.

Tip 3: Show E-Bike Advantage

If you use an e-bike, mention the reduced environmental impact and parking advantage. Venice's eco-conscious community often appreciates this.

Tip 4: Highlight Multi-App Income

If you combine DoorDash with Uber Eats or Instacart, present combined income to meet Venice's higher rent requirements.
